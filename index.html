My Interests

Politics

My interest in Politics was directly translated by my attendance at UWC Maastricht. Indeed, UWC brings together youth from all over the world, in an attempt to overcome their social, political, ideological and economical differences making it a very politically geared environment.

Entrepreneurship

First introduced to Entrepreneurship by my participation to Future Moroccan Entrepreneurs (FME), I have decided to pay forward the knowledge I have gained from the experience by co-founding Yo Peace and being part of the leadership team of the National Entrepreneurship Challenge.

Film and photography

Thanks to my involvement with Poetae Lucis Media, I have been introduced to various media tools in the capturing process as well as the editing process but also online media hosting and managing.

Communication

My involvement in a variety of leadership teams (Amnesty International, MAASMUN, National Entrepreneurship Challenge, Poetae Lucis, Yo Peace...) has helped me get significant experience in communication and team coordination.

Management

Being in leadership teams, I have held a variety of positions: Secretary General, President, Secretary, which all allowed me to explore different needs in organisations and gain a broad understanding of them and their management as a whole.

Languages

The international environment of UWC as well as my bi-national background allowed me to develop a deep interest for a variety of culture and languages. My passion for Latin American Politics pushed me to teach myself Spanish and I have now, after a year, achieved a significant level of fluency that allows me to have a broad understanding of the Latin American culture, politics and variation in language.
